Creamy strawberry cake

Ingredients: Preparation: Tips:
Cake:
  • 4 eggs
  • 140g of caster sugar
  • 2dl self raising flour
  • 25g melted butter

Filling and decoration:
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 6dl double cream
  • 6dl standard milk
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 3tbsp of caster sugar
  • 4tbsp cornstarch

  • 5dl double cream
  • 1kg of strawberries
  • 3dl roasted chopped nuts and almonds (mixed)
  • Icing sugar
 Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven 175Â° and grease and flour the recipient for the batter
  2. Whisk the egg, butter and sugar until it gets puffy. Add the flour and mix it well until the batter gets a nice texture. Empty the mixture into the recipient.
  3. Bake in the lower part of the oven for about 30 minutes. Let the cake rest for a few minutes in the mold. Turn upside down and let it cool on a wire rack.
  4. Once it has cool down, cut the cake in three discs.

Filling and decoration:
  1. Rinse the strawberries. Take half of the strawberries and cut them in slim slices.
  2. Whisk the double cream until it gets foamy.
  3. Mix half of the sliced strawberries with the whisked double cream and spread on one of the bases, place on the next base and spread the rest of the sugared strawberries on it
  4. Place the last base and spread the rest of the double cream around. Decorate with the rest of the strawberries.
 Serving
Add some mint leaves on top!
