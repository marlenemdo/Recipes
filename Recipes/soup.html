Caprese soup

Nothing better than a warm meal in winter ☃️ This tasty soup will melt your soul and take you to the heart of Italy in a second! This recipe is good for four portions.

Ingredients: Preparation: Tips:
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 1 kg mixed tomatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • 1 tablespoon soft brown sugar
  • 50 g basil leaves, plus a few extra to garnish
  • 1½ tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 x 125 g balls of buffalo mozzarella
  1. Preheat the oven to 200ºC.
  2. Cut the garlic in half widthways and place in a large roasting tray with the tomatoes. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of oil and roast in the oven for 25 minutes, or until the tomatoes have burst. Take out the tray and leave to cool slightly.
  3. Squeeze the roasted garlic from its skin and put into a blender with the roasted tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes (drained first), sugar, basil, vinegar and 3 tablespoons of oil.
  4. Blitz until smooth, then transfer to a jug and set aside to come up to room temperature.
  5. Heat a griddle pan and chargrill the sourdough on both sides.
  6. Serve the soup at room temperature in shallow bowls, topped with basil leaves, cracked black pepper and a drizzle of oil, with the bread on the side for dunking.
 Serving
Decorate with some herbs on top!
